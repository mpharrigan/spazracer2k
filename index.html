Spazracer 2000

In middle school I made a game with my friends. Now for the first time – available via the web – spazracer 2000

Directions: Explode the enemy boxes by running into them. Use the arrow keys to move. Time doesn't start until you move. Look out for the power cube! Can't live without it. We highly recommend sound be turned on! Compete with your friends for that ellusive high score. Don't fall off the edge (or do... It's not that big a deal)

Caution: may trigger epilepsy. Disclaimer: Made this in one day despite not knowing javascript.
